The Rizo

Our Pre-Fall 16 collection was an ode to everything Japan and to the power of simple elegance and strength of a woman in control. The new "Rizo" bags are inspired by the rice bags of yesteryear. Pleated and folded, they stand upright easily and their double straps offer multiple carry options.
